Our vision

“Providing recognition to Indian products”.
  • SSC aims at being the industry standard of the future, that differentiates the Indian Products in the global market. SSC has the vision of becoming the mark of indigenous origin and quality.
  • SSC will ensure that the standards are maintained in a fashion that the possession of our certificate will mean the utmost privilege in the eyes of our Government, the Business class and instill satisfactory confidence in the minds of the public as well.
